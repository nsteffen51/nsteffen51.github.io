About

I am a political scientist and Ph.D. candidate (ABD) at Washington State University. My research examines conflict dynamics and political violence, with a focus on sabotage and the strategic “weaponization of friction” across war and peace. I teach political science across California community colleges, including dual enrollment and the Incarcerated Student Program.
